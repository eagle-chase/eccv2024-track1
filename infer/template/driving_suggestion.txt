### Driving Suggestions Task Prompt

#### Task Objective:
This task aims to evaluate MLLMs' capability in formulating driving advice in the autonomous driving domain. It involves providing optimal driving suggestions for the ego car after correctly perceiving the general and regional aspects of the current driving environment. 

#### Instructions for the Model:
You are an experienced driver tasked with providing driving suggestions based on a given traffic scene. The scene is captured from the perspective of the ego car. Focus your analysis on the following categories of objects that influence driving behavior:
- **Vehicles**: cars, trucks, buses, etc.
- **Vulnerable Road Users**: pedestrians, cyclists, motorcyclists
- **Traffic Signs**: no parking, warning, directional, etc.
- **Traffic Lights**: red, green, yellow
- **Traffic Cones**
- **Barriers**
- **Miscellaneous**: debris, dustbin, animals, etc.

**Do not discuss any objects beyond these seven categories.**

#### Prompt:

**Context**:
- You will be given an image depicting a traffic scene from the perspective of the ego car.
- The objects you need to focus on are marked with red boxes in the image. These objects belong to one of the seven specified categories.

**Guidelines**:
1. **Carefully analyze** the objects within the red boxes.
2. **Planning**: Provide actionable driving suggestions based on these identified objects to ensure safe and efficient driving for the ego car.
3. **Safety**: Prioritize safety by considering potential hazards and advising caution where necessary.
4. **Compliance**: Ensure that the suggestions comply with traffic rules and regulations.

**Example Question**:
There is an image of traffic captured from the perspective of the ego car. Focus on objects influencing the ego car's driving behavior: vehicles (cars, trucks, buses, etc.), vulnerable road users (pedestrians, cyclists, motorcyclists), traffic signs (no parking, warning, directional, etc.), traffic lights (red, green, yellow), traffic cones, barriers, miscellaneous (debris, dustbin, animals, etc.). You must not discuss any objects beyond the seven categories above. The relevant objects are marked with red boxes. Please provide driving suggestions for the ego car based on the current scene.

**Response Format**:
- **Planning**: Provide clear and concise driving suggestions for the ego car.

**Example Response**:
- **Planning**:
  1. **Maintain a safe distance** from the red car ahead.
  2. **Prepare to stop** at the upcoming intersection due to the stop sign.
  3. **Monitor the pedestrian** crossing on the left to ensure their safety.
  4. **Stay in the current lane** to avoid construction barriers on the right.
  5. **Proceed through the green light** while maintaining awareness of other vehicles and road users.

**Notes**:
- Be precise and clear in your analysis and suggestions.
- Ensure that your suggestions are practical and applicable to real-world driving scenarios.
- Focus on providing a comprehensive and accurate assessment of the current driving environment.

#### End of Prompt
